Course Curriculum

4 weeks of engaging, age-appropriate activities

Week 1: Discovering My Strengths

  • Activity: "Superhero Me" - Identifying personal strengths
  • Exercise: Positive self-talk practice
  • Group discussion: What makes each of us special
  • Homework: Strength journal

Week 2: Finding My Voice

  • Activity: "Show and Tell" with guided preparation
  • Exercise: Speaking with clarity and volume
  • Role-play: Asking questions in class
  • Homework: Record a short presentation

Week 3: Teamwork & Collaboration

  • Activity: Group storytelling challenge
  • Exercise: Active listening skills
  • Game: "The Confidence Maze" team activity
  • Homework: Family interview project

Week 4: Celebration & Growth

  • Activity: Final presentation showcase
  • Exercise: Recognizing progress and growth
  • Ceremony: Certificates and feedback
  • Take-home: Confidence toolkit

Each weekly session lasts 60 minutes with optional 15-minute Q&A. Homework assignments take approximately 30-45 minutes to complete.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our confidence microcourse

Absolutely! Our program is specifically designed to help shy children gradually build confidence in a safe, supportive environment. We start with low-pressure activities and slowly increase participation at each child's comfort level. Many parents of extremely shy children report significant breakthroughs during the course.

All live sessions are recorded and available for replay for 30 days after the course ends. While we encourage live participation for the full interactive experience, students can still benefit from watching the recordings and completing the activities.

We recommend about 30-45 minutes per week for homework activities. These are designed to be fun and engaging, often involving family participation. The more your child practices between sessions, the more they'll benefit, but all assignments are optional.

For live sessions, you'll need a computer or tablet with a webcam and microphone, and a stable internet connection. We use a secure, child-friendly platform that doesn't require any downloads. For self-paced, you just need any device that can play videos.

For the first session, we encourage parents to be nearby to help with any technical issues. After that, we find children participate more freely when parents aren't visibly present, but you're welcome to stay off-camera in the room if your child prefers. We'll provide you with session summaries so you can follow up at home.
