Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our bootcamp

This bootcamp is designed specifically for Class 10 students preparing for their board exams. Whether you're struggling with certain subjects or just want to ensure you're fully prepared, this intensive revision program will help you maximize your performance.

All sessions are recorded and will be available for participants to review for 7 days after the bootcamp ends. However, we strongly recommend attending live for the interactive elements and immediate doubt resolution.

The bootcamp covers all major Class 10 subjects: Mathematics, Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), Social Studies (History, Geography, Civics), and Languages (English and Hindi/Sanskrit). See the detailed curriculum above for day-wise breakdown.

Unlike regular tuition which focuses on teaching concepts throughout the year, this bootcamp is an intensive revision program designed to consolidate knowledge, practice exam techniques, and identify last-minute improvement areas right before exams.

Sessions run from 4pm to 6pm daily (IST) to accommodate school schedules. The mock test on Day 5 will follow the actual board exam timing (10:30am to 1:30pm).
